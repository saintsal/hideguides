Follow The Chef

This is a list of chefs who've won awards or made a major acheivement - and kept moving. I asked my friend Daro for some insider tips to start the list, and then asked the Tripadvisor community for any more recommendations.

Stvetomir (Niko) Todorov

Winner of Bacchus Restaurant Of The Year, he opened Niko'las.

Sevda Dimitrova

Winner of the second season of Master Chef, she opened Ahora.

Darin Stoikov

Winner of Bacchus' New Bulgarian Cuisine Award when he was Head Chef at Before 10, he opened Daro.

Georgi Boykovski

The Head Chef that started Cosmos, Georgi now runs Bulgar on the top floor of the Marinella Hotel.

Andre Tokev

Andre was shortlisted for Bacchus' Origin Recipe Award while Head Chef at the Hilton, and left to start Andre on ul. Han Krum.
